{B}[About this Mod]{reset}

{B}ParCool MOD{reset} is a mod of Minecraft,
for more {I}Cool{reset} Actions like {I}Parkour{reset}.
Some actions added are inspired by parkour techniques.
This author is {B}NOT{reset} familiar with what called "Parkour" {-}and English{reset}.
so I love your advice to improve this!
======
{B}[Parkour]{reset}

A {I}{B}Parkour{reset} is a kind of sports, organized by {I}Devid Belle{reset}.
The name comes from 'parcours' of French. And the players called 'Traceur'.
There is another similar word, {I}'Free Running'{reset}. This is said to be a word of 'Parkour' translated into English.
======
{B}[Stamina]{reset}

This mod add a concept of Stamina.
Stamina is consumed when you use ParCool Actions.
If you consumed all stamina,
1.You can’t use ParCool Actions.
2.You can’t sprint.
Be careful.
======
{B}[CatLeap]{reset}

CatLeap is a skill used to land on walls,fence or etc...
This is said to be one of basic skills of parkour.
Traceurs absorb an impact of landing with movement of legs.
Let's use also 'Cling to Cliff'!

{blue}<How to use>{reset}
1.do FastRunning
2.quickly sneak
3.quickly cancel sneaking
======
{B}[Crawl]{reset}

You can crawl everywhere.
It’s easier to do something than worry about it.



{blue}<How to use>{reset}
1.press Crawl Key(Normally it’s mapped to C)
======
{B}[Dodge]{reset}

You can move a short distance quickly.
(This is not a skill of the parkour)
You will be very Cool if you should avoid bullets with this!
Cool time exists if players do it three times in short time.

{blue}<How to use>{reset}
1.quickly press Movement Key twice

1.Hold down the dodge roll key and press the moving key
======
{B}[FastRunning]{reset}

It goes without saying.
By the way,do you know? Steve can run as fast as 5.6 meters per seconds.
This is same as a speed of marathon runners.



{blue}<How to use>{reset}
1.continue to press Sprint Key
======
{B}[Cling to Cliff]{reset}

You can cling to cliffs.
This action is enabled even while you have any items in your hands by a update.
You can also climb up onto the cliffs by pressing jump key.


{blue}<How to use>{reset}
1.press GrabCliff Key(Normally it’s mapped to Right Click)
======
{B}[Breakfall]{reset}

Breakfalls are skills for protect our from impact of landing.
One of these is Roll, which change an impact of landing into energy of rolling.
Another is Safety Tap, which absorb an impact by bending your legs.
There are other breakfalls in parkour techniques.
Defaultly player use Safety Tap, but also use Roll by pressing frontward movement key.



{blue}<How to use>{reset}
1.land while you press Breakfall Key(Normally it’s mapped to C)
======
{B}[Vault]{reset}

Vault is skills used to get over obstacles like fence.
There are numerous variations. Why don't you google 'parkour vault'?
The one this mod add is called 'Speed Vault'.



{blue}<How to use>{reset}
1.do FastRunning
2.collide with obstacles
(You can set config to disable this unless you are pressing Vault Key(Normally it's mapped to Right Click)
======

======
{B}[Sliding]{reset}

You can do a slide.



{blue}<How to use>{reset}

======
{B}[Flipping]{reset}

You can do Front or Back Flip.
Defaultly you use Front-Flip but also can use Back-Flip if you are pressing backward movement key.



{blue}<How to use>{reset}
1.Press right and left movement key simultaneously
======
{B}[Wall Slide]{reset}

You can fall slowly by cling to wall.
This action is affected by the slipperiness of blocks.
So players cannot make fall slow with wall of ice block.



{blue}<How to use>{reset}
1.Press Wall Slide Key in air by wall(Normally it's mapped to Right Click)
======
{B}[Horizontal Wall Run]{reset}

You can do wall-running horizontally.
Players cannot continue this over given time.



{B}<How to use>{reset}
1.do Fast-Run in air along the wall
2.Continue to press Wall-Run Key(Normally it's mapped to C)
======
