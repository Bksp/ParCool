{B}[Stamina]{reset}

This mod add a concept of Stamina.
Stamina is consumed when you use ParCool Actions.
If you consumed all stamina,
1.You can’t use ParCool Actions.
2.You can’t sprint.
Be careful.
======

In this Mod,this is implemented simply as a long jump.
